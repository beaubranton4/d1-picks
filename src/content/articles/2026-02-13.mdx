## How We Find These Edges

Quick explainer for the newbies:

1. **Warren Nolan predictions** give us the "true" win probability for each team
2. **Live sportsbook odds** (from DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM) give us the market's implied probability
3. **We compare the two** - when the model thinks a team has a higher chance than the market does, that's our edge
4. **Home field adjustments** - we add 0.5% for home teams, 0.25% for neutral site games
5. **Classification** - edges get categorized into STRONG BET, BET, LEAN, or PASS
